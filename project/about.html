Who am I?

I'm just Federick Gonzalez, and I'm currently a 17 year old student. I go to TERRA Environmental Research Institute, but I graduate on June 4th, 2018, so I can't say I go there for much longer. Once I graduate, I'll be free to say I'm a student of Columbia University!

I applied to Columbia through the Early Decision program, and I recieved my decision on December 14th, 2017. Ever since then, I've counted myself lucky that I applied early, because practically everyone else was worrying where they will go to college, while I already knew. I'm a proud Columbia Lion, and I've been accepted to the Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science, also known as SEAS.



That is me, playing the trombone. I've been playing trombone for 7 years now, but I have been part of the Greater Miami Youth Symhpony for 6 years.
